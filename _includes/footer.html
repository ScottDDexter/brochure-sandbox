Advice for students considering a course of study in the Computer and Information Science department at Brooklyn College. Produced and updated by the faculty of the department. The Brooklyn College Registrar publishes the Undergraduate Bulletin, containing course descriptions, and the Schedule of Classes, which lists courses offered each semester. Not all courses are offered each term. For courses taken before 2010, use this form to match current course numbers with their pre-2010 equivalents The Schedule of Classes published each term lists courses offered in that particular semester. Not all courses are offered each term.
